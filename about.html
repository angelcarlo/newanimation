“Gordon College Office of the Student Welfare and Services Mobile Application (GC OSWS)”

READMORE

The project is concerning the Gordon College Students concerns that could help students to answer their queries. This project also notifies the Gordon College Students about the class suspension updates, activities of the school, etc. The Gordon College Students will surely benefit on the cross platform GC SWS Mobile Application for Gordon College Students to make their life convenient. Instead on going to the Student Welfare and Services Office, the students just have to look for GC SWS Mobile Application to create questions.
